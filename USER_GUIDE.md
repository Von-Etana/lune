## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit Lune Platform**
   - Go to [lune.platform](https://lune.platform)
   - Click "Get Started"

2. **Choose Your Role**
   - **Candidate**: Verify your skills and get certified
   - **Employer**: Find verified talent

3. **Sign Up**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a secure password (min. 8 characters)
   - Provide your full name
   - Verify your email

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add professional information
   - Upload a video introduction (optional)
   - Set your preferences

## Understanding Assessments

### How Assessments Work

1. **AI-Generated**: Each assessment is unique
2. **Skill-Specific**: Tailored to the chosen skill
3. **Difficulty-Appropriate**: Matches selected level
4. **Proctored**: AI monitors for integrity
5. **Instant Results**: Immediate evaluation

### Assessment Structure

**Theory Section (40%)**
- 5-10 multiple choice questions
- Conceptual understanding
- Best practices
- Real-world scenarios

**Coding Section (60%)**
- 1-2 coding challenges
- Starter code provided
- Test cases to pass
- Code quality matters

### Scoring

- **0-69%**: Not Passed - Try again after studying
- **70-79%**: Passed - Good understanding
- **80-89%**: Passed - Strong skills
- **90-100%**: Passed - Excellent mastery

### Proctoring System

Our AI proctoring ensures integrity:

**What We Monitor:**
- Tab switches
- Copy-paste events
- Multiple faces detected
- Suspicious patterns

**What We DON'T Do:**
- Record video/audio
- Store biometric data
- Share monitoring data
- Invade privacy

**Flags Don't Mean Failure:**
- Minor flags are normal
- Severe violations affect score
- Honest mistakes are forgiven
- Pattern matters, not single events

## Blockchain Certificates

### What is a Blockchain Certificate?

A blockchain certificate is:
- **Immutable**: Cannot be altered or faked
- **Permanent**: Stored forever on blockchain
- **Verifiable**: Anyone can verify authenticity
- **Portable**: Take it anywhere
- **Trustworthy**: Cryptographically secure

### PWRCHAIN Technology

Lune uses PWRCHAIN for certificates:
- Fast transactions
- Low fees
- Environmentally friendly
- Public verification
- Global accessibility

### Certificate Details

Each certificate contains:
- Your name
- Skill verified
- Score achieved
- Difficulty level
- Timestamp
- Unique blockchain hash

### Verifying Certificates

**As a Certificate Holder:**
1. Go to your Certifications
2. Click "View on Blockchain"
3. See transaction on PWRCHAIN explorer

**As an Employer:**
1. Receive certificate link

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Lune free to use?**
A: Yes! Candidates can take assessments and earn certificates for free. Employers pay for premium features.

**Q: How long does an assessment take?**
A: Typically 30-45 minutes depending on difficulty.

**Q: Can I retake an assessment?**
A: Yes, but wait 7 days between attempts for the same skill.

**Q: Are certificates really on blockchain?**
A: Yes! Every certificate is minted on PWRCHAIN and publicly verifiable.

### For Candidates

**Q: What if I fail an assessment?**
A: Study the feedback and try again in 7 days. No penalty for failing.

**Q: Can employers see failed assessments?**
A: No, only passed assessments and certificates are visible.

**Q: How do I improve my match score?**
A: Take more assessments, improve scores, and keep your profile updated.

**Q: Is my webcam recording saved?**
A: No, we only analyze in real-time. No video is stored.

### For Employers

**Q: How accurate is the AI matching?**
A: Very accurate - it's based on verified skills, not self-reported.

**Q: Can I contact candidates directly?**
A: Yes, through the platform messaging system.

**Q: How do I verify a certificate?**
A: Click the certificate and select "Verify on Blockchain."

**Q: What if a candidate cheated?**
A: Our AI proctoring detects cheating. Flagged submissions are reviewed.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Do I need a webcam?**
A: Yes, for taking proctored assessments.

**Q: What if my internet disconnects?**
A: Your progress is auto-saved. Resume when reconnected.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices.
